Question formats

Single-correct (40%)

Pick one of four options. Standard "which of the following is correct" pattern.

Multi-correct (30%)

Four or five options, multiple correct. Partial credit: correct picks minus wrong picks, divided by total correct, floored at 0.

Negative-phrasing (30%)

"Which is incorrect / wrong / impossible." Can be single or multi. Heavily used by the real exam.
